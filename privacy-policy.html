Privacy Policy - Unlocl

Last updated: 07 December 2025

Unlocl operates the Unlocl mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our App. By using Unlocl, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Phone number, email address, name, and other details you provide when you sign up or update your profile.
  • Location Information:
    • Approximate and precise location when you allow location access (for showing nearby shops, delivery availability, etc.).
    • If background location is enabled and used, location may be collected even when the app is in the background, for features such as real-time order tracking or delivery-related services.
  • Camera & Media: Photos and videos you capture or select to upload for your profile, shop images, product images, or status/features using the camera or gallery.
  • Microphone & Audio: Audio that you record within the app (e.g., voice messages, voice chat). We do not record audio unless you explicitly start a voice action or recording feature.
  • Device & Log Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, IP address, language, and usage data such as in-app actions, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Transactional & Order Information: Information related to orders placed through the App, such as items, shop details, price, date/time, and related status.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies (if applicable): We may use cookies or similar identifiers for analytics, security, and improving user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • Creating and managing your account.
  • Showing nearby/local shops and relevant content based on your location.
  • Enabling placing and managing orders with local shops.
  • Providing features like photo upload, status updates, and voice messages/voice chat.
  • Improving, maintaining, and optimizing App performance and user experience.
  • Monitoring usage, preventing fraud, and ensuring security of the platform.
  • Providing customer support and responding to your queries.
  • Complying with legal obligations when required.

Permissions We Use

The App may request the following Android permissions for the reasons described below:

  • Internet: To connect to our servers, load content, authenticate users, process orders, and communicate with shops.
  • Location (Fine, Coarse, and possibly Background): To detect your location, show nearby shops, and support delivery and location-based services. If background location is used, it is only for features that require continuous tracking (for example, live order tracking) and will be requested separately according to platform rules.
  • Camera: To allow you to capture and upload photos or videos for your profile, products, shops, or status.
  • Photos, Media & Files (Read Media Images/Video/Audio, legacy Storage): To allow you to select existing photos, videos, or audio files from your device to upload inside the app.
  • Microphone (Record Audio): To record voice messages, audio notes, or use voice chat features. Audio is captured only when you explicitly start a recording/voice feature.

You can manage these permissions from your device settings. However, disabling certain permissions may limit some functionalities of the App.
